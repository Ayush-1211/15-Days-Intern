About Us

Located on the rooftop of a beautiful Indo-Baroque heritage mansion dating from the 1920s, namely the House of MG, Ayush's Restauranat offers the very best of fine dining in the middle of the old town. There is no menu in the traditional sense. Instead, all throughout the meal, attentive waiters keep bringing a succession of delicious vegetarian Gujarati food to the table which together constitute the Thali. Guests are free to eat as much as they want from their favourite dishes, and drink non-alcoholic drinks which are included in the price. For a contemporary Gujarati experience at its best, set in an impressive decor, Ayush's Restaurant is the right place.
